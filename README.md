Title: Soko Safi AI – Closing the Loop on Market Waste and Food Security

In the bustling markets of Ahero and Kibuye, Kisumu County, a silent crisis unfolds daily. High temperatures and lack of proper storage lead to massive post-harvest losses for smallholder traders. When produce rots, livelihoods wither. Traders, forced to sell at "distress prices" or dump waste into drainages, face a vicious cycle of poverty.

Soko Safi AI is a circular-economy solution designed to disrupt this cycle. We provide cost-effective, cold storage hubs powered by an on-site biodigester that converts market organic waste into clean energy. By implementing a "Waste-as-Currency" model, we enable traders to exchange their unsellable produce for cold storage credits.

Beyond cold storage, we are cleaning the market environment and producing high-grade organic fertilizer (bio-slurry) to restore soil health in local farms. Powered by a bilingual WhatsApp-based AI assistant, Soko Safi is not just a storage facility; it is a sustainable ecosystem that turns waste into wealth, ensuring fresher food for consumers and dignity for the trader.
